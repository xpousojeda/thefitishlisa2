About Me

Hi, I’m Lisa Swanepoel! Your friendly CPA-turned-purpose-driven coach.

For over a decade, I worked in the accounting and finance world—leading teams, improving processes, and navigating the sometimes chaotic waters of numbers and audits. As a Certified Public Accountant, I prided myself on producing results, staying organized, and helping others succeed. But deep down, I always felt there was more to life than spreadsheets and systems.

I’ve always believed in serving others, whether it was helping my team solve a tricky problem or showing up for friends and family. I learned the power of encouragement, the importance of grace, and the magic of showing up; even on the hard days. Through it all, I discovered that my real passion wasn’t just about finances. It was about helping people create lives they actually want to live.

That’s where The Fitish Lisa was born. Not out of perfection—but out of realness! Out of learning to balance health, family, work, and purpose with a little flexibility and a lot of intention.

Today, I combine everything I’ve learned; from accounting to coaching to motherhood—to help others feel empowered, stay accountable, and build lives that feel as good on the inside as they look on the outside. Whether I’m speaking to a room full of professionals, leading a coaching session, or writing a blog post, I’m always showing up as myself: imperfect, authentic, and deeply committed to your success.

Thanks for being here, I’m honored to walk alongside you!
